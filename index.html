What is gimworld?

gimworld is a builder-focused community where Gimkit Creative creators share their work, learn techniques, troubleshoot, and collaborate on projects. Whether you're building your first map or you're a seasoned creator, this is your space to connect with other builders.

Who runs it? Leo The Great (Leonel Omovie), CEO of PixeWorld.

How to Join

  1. Visit the gimworld Padlet
  2. Check out the Community Rules & FAQ
  3. Start sharing your creations and connecting with other builders
