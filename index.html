What is GimWorld?

GimWorld is where Gimkit Creative builders come together to share their work, learn new techniques, troubleshoot challenges, and collaborate on projects. Whether you're building your first game or refining advanced mechanics, our community provides support, inspiration, and resources to help you succeed.

Our mission is to foster a welcoming, respectful space for educators and students who want to create engaging educational games using Gimkit Creative. Share your builds, discover innovative design patterns, get feedback, and connect with fellow builders around the world.

How to Join

Getting started with GimWorld is easy! Follow these simple steps to join our active builder community:

  1. Visit the GimWorld Padlet
  2. Review the Community Rules & FAQ to understand our guidelines
  3. Start sharing your creations and connecting with other builders

Community Guidelines

We maintain a positive, inclusive environment for all members. Here's what we stand for:

Core Values:

  • ✅Be respectful and supportive of all community members
  • 🎨Share your builds, tutorials, and help others learn
  • 💡Encourage collaboration and creative problem-solving
  • 🚫No spam or self-promotion outside of your creations
  • 📚Keep discussions constructive and on-topic
